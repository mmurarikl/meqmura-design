About Mequila

From studio practice to structured code.

I began in fine art, studying painting and drawing with a focus on composition, restraint, and intentional mark-making. That foundation continues to shape how I approach digital design. To me, every element has weight, every decision has purpose.

After serving overseas in the military, I developed a deep respect for structure, discipline, and clarity. Those principles now guide my design process. I build with semantic HTML, organized CSS, and deliberate interaction and not decoration for decoration’s sake.

My work sits at the intersection of art and functionality. I’m interested in how visual systems translate into usable interfaces, and how thoughtful design can create inclusive, accessible experiences for diverse audiences.

Background

  • Bachelor of Fine Arts (Painting + Drawing)
  • Bachelor of Science in Graphic Information Technology (Front-End Web Development)
  • International experience (Spain + Africa)

Focus

  • Structured visual systems
  • Thoughtful front end builds
